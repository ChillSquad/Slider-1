Orange

They are rich in vitamin C, flavonoids, carotenoids, sources of pectin and contain minerals such as potassium, magnesium and phosphorus. The benefits of oranges include strengthening the immune system, supporting cardiovascular health, fighting aging, and improving digestion. Orange fragrance can help in the fight against stress and improve emotional state, thanks to the production of serotonin, the "hormone of happiness".

Green

Apples contain a large amount of fiber, pectin, vitamins C, group B, provitamin A. Fruits are rich in polyphenols with antioxidant properties, they have few calories and practically no sodium, fat and cholesterol. This is a product that benefits health by saturating the body with useful substances and strengthening the immune system. The main benefit of apples is a positive effect on the functioning of the gastrointestinal tract.

Blue

Blueberries are very useful for the eyes — they improve blood supply to the retina and sharpen vision, but the main benefit of blueberries for the body is the high content of antioxidants — anthocyanins, which act at the cellular level, rejuvenating organs and stopping the aging process. Another valuable property of blueberries is to stop the growth of tumors, which reduces the likelihood of cancer.
